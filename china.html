china

China is huge, colorful, and full of contrasts. From quiet mountain villages to buzzing megacities, it's a country where ancient traditions meet modern life every day. Traveling here means tasting endless flavors, hearing many languages, and seeing landscapes that change completely from one province to the next. It's a place that never stops surprising me. Pandas in Chengdu

chengdu

Chengdu feels warm and alive — the kind of place that makes you slow down and smile. It's home to China's beloved giant pandas, where you can watch them nap, play, and munch on bamboo all day. And when evening comes, nothing beats a bubbling pot of spicy hotpot shared with friends. In Chengdu, life is never in a rush — it's all about good food, gentle pandas, and easy laughter.

hangzhou

Hangzhou feels like a poem written in water and mist. The calm beauty of West Lake reflects willow trees, old bridges, and boats drifting softly across the water. Not far away, Lingyin Temple hides among green hills, its incense smoke rising quietly through the air. And in winter, when the plum blossoms bloom in the Xixi Wetlands, their sweet scent fills the cold breeze — a gentle reminder that spring is never far away.

The night view on qinhuai River

nanjing

Nanjing carries the weight of history and the calm of a city that remembers. Once the capital of several dynasties, it still keeps echoes of the past in its ancient walls and quiet temples. Walking along the Qinhuai River at night, lanterns reflect on the water like small pieces of time. In spring, the plum blossoms around the Ming Xiaoling Tomb bring color and life to the old stone paths — a soft reminder that even in a city of memories, beauty always returns.

lijiang

Lijiang feels like a dream caught between mountains and clouds. The water of Blue Moon Lake is so clear it looks unreal — a mirror of turquoise and light surrounded by snow peaks. In the old town, narrow stone lanes wind past wooden houses and flowing canals, where music drifts softly from open windows. At night, the air fills with the scent of food and the sound of laughter. Lijiang is a place where time slows down, and every corner feels touched by a quiet kind of magic.
